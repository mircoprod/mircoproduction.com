Mir&Co Production

MIR&CO PRODUCTION was founded in 2014 after the first Russian invasion in Ukraine. Then a like-minded group of cinematographers focused their work on making documentaries mainly on military themes to share important events with the world. All of the studio's films have participated in a number of festivals and broadcasted on TV channels in different countries.

Since 2020, MIR&CO PRODUCTION has also started creating feature films, music videos and advertising. The specialty of our company is the author's view, which reveals any story like a movie.

Today the main goal of the studio is not only to continue telling the painful stories of the war, which continues to this day, but also to share the multifaceted Ukrainian culture and develop Ukrainian cinema on the world stage.
